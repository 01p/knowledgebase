# Data Types in Agrosystem Science

What data types exist in agrosystem science? What do I have to consider when collecting and publishing data – including the legal perspective?

[Here](https://doi.org/10.4126/FRL01-006461782) we give a short overview of relevant data types and their peculiarities regarding research data management.
With hints to our expertises in the DSSC and further ressources.

_Which data types are covered?_  

- Soil data
- Field data, in general
- Genetic data (“omics data”)
- Phenotyping data
- Field robotics and sensor data
- Big geodata
- Legal Framework

_Which target groups are the Data Factsheets for?_  
The Data Factsheets are for anyone who wants to get an initial overview of the topics covered by agrosystems research and FAIRagro as a consortium.

_How are the data types described?_  
Each data type is briefly explained. We provide information on the specifics of research data management as well as selected repositories and other resources for searching and publishing the data types.
You will also find information on our expertise in the DSSC and the target group for which our services are intended.

_Did we miss something?_  
If you know of other relevant data types and/or resources, please send your feedback to the [Helpdesk](mailto:dataservice@fairagro.net).

[Link to the Document (Publisso)](https://doi.org/10.4126/FRL01-006461782)
